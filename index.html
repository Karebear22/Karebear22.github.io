Hot Chocolate

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 3 cups
Cocoa Powder 2 TBSP
Sugar 2 TBSP
Vanilla 1/2 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp

Step 1: Heat the milk in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring regularly and being carful not to bring to a boil.

Step 2: Once the milk has started to warm up a bit, add the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt, stirring vigorously until everything is dissolved.

Step 3: Once the hot cocoa is heated to your liking, remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Divide into 2 mugs and enjoy!

Best Easy Brownies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Yield: 12 medium brownies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 10 TBSP
Semisweet chocolate 4 ounces
Sugar 1 Cup
Eggs 2 large plus 1 yolk
vanilla 1 tsp
all-purpose flour 3/4 cup
cocoa powder 1/4 cup
salt 1/4 tsp
chocolate chips 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a metal 8-inch square pan with parchment paper.

Step 2:In a large microwave-safe bowl combine the chocolate and butter. Microwave in 30-second bursts, stirring between each burst, until the mixture is melted and smooth. Add the sugar to the hot butter mixture and whisk vigorously until combined. Allow to cool until just barely warm.

Step 3: Add in eggs, yolk, and vanilla extract and whisk for about 1 minute, or until very well combined. This helps create that shiny crust on top.

Step 4:Use a rubber spatula to stir in flour, cocoa powder, and salt until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips.

Step 5:Pour into prepared pan and smooth out. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick entered in the center comes out with moist crumbs. Let cool in pan 30 minutes before slicing.

Source: Handle the Heat

Grandma's Double Chocolate Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Yield: 4 dozen cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1 cup (2 sticks)
Sugar 1 1/2 cups
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 2 tsps
Flour 2 cups
Cocoa powder 2/3 cups
Baking soda 3/4 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Step 2:In a large bowl, beat butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla with and electric mixer until light and fluffy.

Step 3: Stir together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt; add to butter mixture.

Step 4:Stir in chocolate chips and drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.

Step 5:Bake 8-10 minutes or just until set. Cool slightly; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely and enjoy!

Source: My grandma!
